Team Design Project Study

In the course, we are required to design an intelligent car during one semester.

My group member and I build our smart rover with functions of line tracking, color recognition, distance measurement, object delivery, inter-board communication, dynamic self- adaptive direction control and turning on the spot. The electrical hierarchy of this vehicle consists of STM32 and OpenMV together with their corresponding peripherals.

To achieve high efficiency, our group is divided into two sub-teams: Car Group and Image Group. I was the coordinator of Car Group, leading four members from different major. The work of the Car Group is to control the car movement, covering the work of assembling the car, wiring, driving circuit design and manufacturing, PID algorithm, etc. [Link]
